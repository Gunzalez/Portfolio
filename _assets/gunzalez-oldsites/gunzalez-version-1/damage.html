Damage

Yes, the very same Damage, the only black boy band in the UK, and they ain't too bad either, I quite like the chaps. It was quite an honour to work on this web site, it was my first time doing stuff that would be seen by all of the UK.

Loads of pressure because the launch had to coincide with the release of a new single, and posters were all over the UK with the URL in bold letters, ...we got it out and done in the end, the first version the not look this good, futhure refinement was needed.

I was a team of 3, with yours truly was project leader. It was also my first meaty / substantial peice of work since I joined Hypnosis media and I was nervous not to fail, thank god I did not, ....well I hope not.

While there was some debate on the use of animated gtaphics for the navigation bar, some said to use Flash, I insisted on non Flash elements, and as project leader, what I say stays, you got that!. Link to the web site by clicking on the image above.

People keep asking me if while working on this did I get to hang out with the boys, ...well the answer is no. Just their managment and other Damage personnel. Also had access to some pretty cool Damage stuff which of course never be made public. Big up to the boys.
