Self Trade

Yep, this was my first proper job. You might notice the thumbnail on main page defers from this one here. This is because the web site has been seriously updated since then, it's got a complete new look.

The company 'Self Trade' specialisez in online share dealing. It was a massive project, with teams working on it here in London and Australia or France or Sweden or somewhere (I forget), and the team in London was further divided into two, Desgin and Development.

A company called 'Light & Coley' were called in to desgin the front end, which included some Flash animation, ...actually quite a lot of animation. And that was my part I worked with Flash on the web site. Light & Coley called my in on a contract.

This included building some homepage animation and some online demo showing the public how easy it is to by and sell share online. Spent loads of late nights getting things done on time, but a very enlightening experience it was.

An eye opener, a taste of real life work, deadlines, budgets etc. However I did get bitten on the pay side coz I never had my own ltd company, I got paid via some other company and they taxed my ass to no end. Hence my present preference to permanent positions.

I met a few people here and they were all quite nice, I got learn new things, discover different music (Moby just came out and I fell in love with Moby back then). But I still did not feel I belonged, it's that last difference between permanenet and contract positions, coz it's at the back of ones mind that one has to leave in a few months or so.

Nonetheless, I do not regret a single moment of it, it's all a learning experience, init.
