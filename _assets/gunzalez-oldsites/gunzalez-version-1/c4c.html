c4C: concept for Christ

This here cute little number as you might have guessed was a web site for a religious group, it was one I took on on my own just to add to my online examples as I did not have many at the time.

While it is obviously not complex in anyway it did refresh my knowledge of certain areas of HTML, ...things like Imgae mapping, to use or not to use framesets, and how not to kill a web site with crappy color choices.

A lot more content was intended but as project neared completion, disagreements on bandwidth price and thus contents would ensue, and consequently it remains as empty as it does today, as the day that I finished it.

On the bright side, I had yet another thing to point at on the web and say 'hey, look at what I've done'. If me remembers correctly some parts of the site do not function as I knew no Perl or any other ServerSide technology at the time, I hope they have fixed it.

Oh, and by the way I still know very little Perl, ASP is my baby now, ...yes, that's right, another minion of ol' uncle Gates. But I do know some PHP too.
