BUPA (/wellness)

This was part of the Bupa Intranet, and so is not available online. It is a web based publication for the employees of one of the many internal departments that make up Bupa, this one was called Wellness.

Design wise it was not challenging, I had to work within understandably tight desgin guidlines, but fun to do due to the people I worked with.

I did however get to use my customised DHTML script, downloaded free off the internet, and hence a valuable new technique was learned. I used it for the DropDown menu navigation facility.

While I do not have more than screen shots to show here I do have it stored on a floppy disk to bring in to any interview if needed.
