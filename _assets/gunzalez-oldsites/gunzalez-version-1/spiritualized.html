Spiritualized

One of the many projects which I can say I enjoyed a great deal working on was the Spiritualized web site, another band web site, but one which stood out in it's clean, clear and effective desgin.

Athough this was sorta my baby and I did a huge chunk of it, this web site was developed as always, by a team. A work mate would desgin while I would build, and it was a challenge to convert to HTML, especially the Discography page with it's DHTML Navigation.

However I liked it so much, I used it on my web site here. The animation however is only DHTML and the way Dreamweaver writes it some browers do not quite work with it, that's why it looks and works differently in different browsers, IE5+ on PC being the best combination.

For this one, I was there from the very beginning and I'm quite pround of it too.
